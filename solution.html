Pure & Simple

As the project began to take shape, I realized I wanted the pages that had were light on information to be more colorful and eyecatching.

On the other hand, I wanted the info dense pages to be minimalist with call to actions highlighted and in focus. The ‘catalogue’ page in particular needed to be highly readable and organized.

I added a feature to use a modal to see detailed descriptions of products in a focused way. Keeping the pages uncluttered will also cut down on mistakes made by users within the ordering sections.
